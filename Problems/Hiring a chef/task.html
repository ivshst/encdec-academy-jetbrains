Hiring a chef Open on JetBrains Academy

Imagine that you are an HR manager at a restaurant and your task is to hire a chef. To do that, you need to collect some preliminary data about the candidates. You have a special form that includes 5 fields: first name, age, stage of education, years of experience, cuisine preference. 
Your program should read all the words (or numbers) from the five lines and output "The form for name is completed".


Sample Input:
Eugene
33
secondary
8
fusion
Sample Output:
The form for Eugene is completed


Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 8 seconds
